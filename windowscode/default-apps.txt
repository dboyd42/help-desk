Change Default Apps
-------------------

If prgm/app is Windows approved/MS Store:

    Settings > Default apps > by{filetype,protocol,app}

Else:

    1) Create a new text document
    2) Change the extension to desired filetype.
    3) Rt-click > Open With ... > Choose another app
    4) More apps
    5) Choose app/prgm
    6) [Checkbox] Always use this app to open .<ext> files
