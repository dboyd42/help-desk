Cannot preview images in windows explorer or my computer "pictures" folder
===========================================================================

You may follow the below steps and check if the option is unchecked –

    Open Windows Explorer window. In the menu click on the tab Tools (If not visible click the ALT key for the menu to get displayed).
    Now click on Folder options. In the opened window go to the view tab and uncheck the option “Always show icons, never thumbnails”. Now click Apply and OK. 

Now check if you are able to preview the pictures.
